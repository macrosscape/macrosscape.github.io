Macross 7 Coloring Book

Published by Seika Note in 1995. Two-page spreads have already been edited together, removing staples and spine creases. Pages are displayed out of print order in order to accomidate changes in page orientation. If you color in a page, feel free to email it to us at firebombersroom@gmail. If we get enough submissions we'll host them all together in a gallery. Submissions can be digital or analog.
